About the Forest

Spanning 400 acres, Witherton Forest offers fun for all the family. Witherton forest can offer
200 acres of woodland suitable for walking and cycling with clear routes and picnic areas,
a flying Squirrel adventure ropes course, crazy Paintball and a children’s zoo with cafe.
The forest has easy access by car and bus/train, meaning getting here will be no issue.

New for 2016, Witherton has a new attraction to add to their already exciting mix. Visitors
can now try out Hydro Zorbing for £45 per session. They can have the washing machine
experience between April and October.

Getting to Witherton Forest

Situated to the north of Stoke-on-Trent, there is easy access from both the M6 and the new
A50 dual carriageway. Manchester, Birmingham, Wolverhampton and Derby are all within
1 hours travel.
